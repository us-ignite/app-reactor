Smart Gigabit Community Ecosystem

Key features separate communities ready to take the lead
in the emerging gigabit economy. Ecosystems that provide services, attract
businesses, and enable growth have these features in common.

  • Gigabit Speeds

    Ultra-Fast networks that exceed the limits of current broadband technologies enable sharing more information than ever before, at faster speeds, in all directions.

  • Real-Time Responses

    Services can recieve replies from systems on the edge of the community network with no perceptable delay, providing the same interactive as being there in person.

  • Intelligent Networks

    Software Defined Networks can adapt to changing conditions in real-time and intelligently route traffic to isolate critical infrastructure to the right members.

  • Local Traffic Exchanges

    Gigabit network traffic never leaves the region and local network providers exchange local traffic freely for the shortest routes and response times possible.

  • Innovation Incubator

    Local developers connect and communicate freely with one another, they understand how to access local infrastructure to develop new ideas and have a way to incubate their applicaitons locally in edge-compute hosts.

  • Edge Computing

    Compute resources are integrated into the community infrastructure and available to innovators. Local data-centers, community academic or government clusters, and compute capable embedded devices all allow smart applications to run at or near the point of interaction for ultra-fast response times.
