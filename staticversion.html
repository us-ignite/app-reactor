Smart Gigabit Community Echosystem

Some key features separate communities ready to take the lead
in the emerging gigabit economy. Proviging services, attracting
buisnesses, and enabling growth have these things in common.

  • Gigabit Speeds

    Fast networks that execeed the limits of current broadband enable sharing more information than ever before, at faster speeds, in all directions.

  • Real-Time Responses

    Using regional compute resources enable users and services to recieve a reply from the edge of thier community with no noticeable delay.

  • Intelligent Networks

    Software Defined Networks can adapt to changing conditions in realtime and intelligently route traffic to isolate critical infrastructure to the right members.

  • Local Traffic Exchanges

    Gigabit network traffic never leaves the region and providers exchange local traffic freely for the shortest routes and response times possible.

  • Innovation Incubator

    Local developers connect and communicate freely with one another, they understand how to accesss local infrastructure to develop new ideas and have a way to incubate their applicaitons locally in edge-compute hosts.

  • Edge Computing

    Compute resourses are integrated into the community infrastructure and available to innovators. Local datacenters, community academic or government clusters, and compute capable embedded devices all allow smart applications to run at or near the point of interaction for ultra-fast response times.
